# ⌨️ Typing Trainer

A simple, beautiful web app to improve your typing speed in **English** and **Arabic**.

No installation. No accounts. No backend. Just open the page and start typing.

---

## 🌟 What is this?

Typing Trainer is a lightweight, single-page web app that gives you 10 random
sentences to type. It tracks your time and your mistakes, then shows you a
clean summary of how you did. Train as many times as you like.

It's built to be friendly for beginners while still feeling smooth and
satisfying to use every day.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🌐 Two languages: English and العربية (with full RTL support)
- 🎲 10 random sentences each round, picked from a large pool
- 🎯 Live feedback per character: correct, wrong, current, untyped
- ⏱ Timer that starts on your first keystroke
- 🔢 Smart mistake counter — fix a typo with Backspace and the mistake is forgiven
- 🔔 A pleasant bell sound when you finish a sentence with zero mistakes
- 📊 Results page with total time, average time, and per-sentence breakdown
- 📱 Responsive design for desktop and mobile
- 🎨 Modern UI with gradients, soft shadows, and smooth animations

---

## 🚀 How to use it

1. Download or clone this repository.
2. **Double-click `index.html`** to open it in your browser.
3. Pick **English** or **العربية**.
4. Start typing. Letters turn green when correct, red when wrong.
5. Use **Backspace** to fix mistakes.
6. After 10 sentences, see your results and click **Train Again** to play another round.

That's it. No setup, no terminal, no dependencies.
